Overview

Purpose

To present the adventure of water rafting to any possible customer in a interesting, easy and exciting way and to facilitate the access to our services

Audience

Who are our target customers? They are people who love outdoors activity and wants to experience nature and adventure, they can come as the parents of a family, young people who wants to experience it with friends, or different type of groups like business class or student groups

What do they want? They want to experience adventure in the nature but also be sure of their safety and care from the managing party

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? where the activies take place, what is planned for the adventure, prices, if there is some equipment needed, who can participate, the team in charge and the experience of the company in the activity
